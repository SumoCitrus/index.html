General Medication Directions
WMC Gastroenterology Pre-Operative Instructions
⚠️
Please disregard if not taking any medications listed below

PLEASE CONSULT YOUR PRESCRIBING PHYSICIAN ABOUT TAKING ALL PRESCRIBED MEDICATIONS. Below are general directions. You must consult your physician to confirm how these medications should be adjusted prior to procedure.

💊 Diabetes / Weight Loss Medications

Diabetes Pills

Stop 4 days prior to procedure:canagliflozin (Invokana), dapagliflozin (Farxiga), empagliflozin (Jardiance), ertugliflozin (Stelgato)
Day before procedure:Take the AM dose but hold PM dose (night before procedure).
Day of procedure:Hold AM dose of diabetes medication.

Insulin

Day before procedure:Take half the usual dose at night.
Day of procedure:Hold AM dose of insulin.

GLP-1 / Weight Loss Medications

Daily RYBELSUS:HOLD on the DAY OF PROCEDURE.
Weekly GLP-1 injections — VICTOZA, OZEMPIC, WEGOVY, MOUNJARO, TRULICITY:HOLD the injection the WEEK prior to procedure.
🩸 Blood Thinners / Anticoagulants / Antiplatelets
If on any blood thinners or anticoagulants (coumadin, warfarin, Plavix, Eliquis, Pradaxa, etc.):PLEASE CONTACT YOUR PHYSICIAN TO CONFIRM WHETHER AND WHEN TO STOP YOUR MEDICATIONS.
💊 Aspirin
If your Doctor prescribed Aspirin:Do NOT stop the Aspirin.
PLEASE CONSULT YOUR PRESCRIBING PHYSICIAN ABOUT TAKING ALL PRESCRIBED MEDICATIONS. The above are general directions only. You must consult your physician to confirm how these medications should be adjusted prior to procedure.

🍋Clear fruit juices: apple juice, white grape juice, white cranberry, lemonade
🍧Sorbets: Italian ice, Flavored ices
🫧Coconut water

⏰ Bowel Prep Instructions

Please stay well hydrated throughout your prep.

⏰ 1:00 PM — Dulcolax
💊

Dulcolax (Bisacodyl) — 2 Tablets

At 1PM take 2 Dulcolax (Bisacodyl) tablets by mouth.

⏰ 6:00 PM — MiraLAX
🫙

MiraLAX 238g in 64 oz of liquid

Mix the entire 238g bottle of MiraLAX in 64 oz of Gatorade/Pedialyte or any non-red/orange/purple clear liquid. Drink one 8 oz glass of solution every 15 minutes until completed.

1
Mix MiraLAX into 64 oz of clear liquid

Pour the entire 238g bottle of MiraLAX into 64 oz of Gatorade, Pedialyte, or any non-red/orange/purple clear liquid. Stir until fully dissolved. Keep cold.

🫙
2
Drink one 8 oz glass every 15 minutes

Starting at 6 PM, drink one 8 oz glass (240 mL) every 15 minutes. Set a timer and keep going until all 64 oz are finished.

⏱️
3
Stay close to the bathroom

You will need to use the bathroom many times. This is completely normal. Stay home and close to the toilet.

🚽
💡
Tips to Help

Keep the mixture cold — it tastes better chilled. Drink through a straw. Suck on hard candy between glasses. Stay well hydrated throughout your prep.

🎯
